Turning a Problem into a Promise

It is our job as businesses to solve problems. That and to generate money. However, if we are solving a problem, we want people to know we are solving their problem. The best way to do that online? Headers. The h1 tag is the giant neon sign saying "We can help you!" After that, it's the job of the paragraph to turn the problem into a promise.

The p tag is a powerful tool in relaying additional information to consumers. It allows explaination. What is a promise if there is no evidence to back up the claim? People will disregard it and you will be left with no one. The paragraph creates and explains your promise.
